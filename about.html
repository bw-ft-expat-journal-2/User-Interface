Bringing the world to you

From the beginning Expat Journal has been about bringing the world to your fingertips. Whether you're down town or far out where there's not another person in sight, we want to give you the best experience possible. We offer our consumers long lasting impressions about things that they love!

Redefine adventure

If you are traveling and want to share photos, or want to frequently update your family back home then we've got you covered. We are redefining adventure with and easy-to-use upload dashboard. Share your album and have friends contribute for safe keeping of your long lasting adventures. Our app is mainly for social media but you do have the option of saving private memories for a select group of people.

A team that cares

Our team works day and night to bring consumers the best oppertunities to hold onto memories or share them with the world. Our team of 8 dedicated coding wizards are at your disposal with knowledge of front-end, back-end and architectual development!
